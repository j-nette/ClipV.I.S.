Talk to it

Natural voice summons any model. A real LLM maps what you say to the right 3D asset + narration.

Webcam hand-tracking (MediaPipe): point to highlight, pinch to grab & rotate, two-hand pull-apart to zoom.

A four-camera volumetric pinwheel projects into the pyramid — visible 360°. Exploded view, x-ray & wireframe modes.
